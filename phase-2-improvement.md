# Lecsy Phase 2 Improvement Plan

## Nice-to-Have Features

- **1. Simple Search**
  - Enable basic Ctrl+F style search within transcript.

- **2. Timestamps**
  - Show what time each sentence was said (if possible).

- **3. Speaker Labels**
  - Identify different speakers (e.g., "Speaker 1:", "Speaker 2:") if possible.

- **4. Highlight & Copy**
  - Provide an easy way to select and copy parts of the transcript.

- **5. History/Library**
  - Keep all past transcriptions organized for the user.

---

## Additional Enhancements

- Add testimonials and social proof (e.g., "10,000+ lectures transcribed").
- Provide a sample transcript and/or video demo.
- Expand FAQ and support resources.
- Improve analytics and event tracking for advanced usage insights.

---

## Summary

- Phase 2 focuses on advanced usability, organization, and trust-building features.
- These features are not essential for launch but will greatly improve the product experience and retention.
